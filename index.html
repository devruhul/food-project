My Recipies

A recipe is a set of instructions that describes how to prepare or make something, especially a dish of prepared food. Earlier recipes often included much less information, serving more as a reminder of ingredients and proportions for someone who already knew how to prepare the dish

Chicken Salna

Chicken Salna is a well-known street side dish in southern parts of Tamil Nadu. Simple, quick and easy-to-make, salna is a thin gravy usually made with mutton or chicken stock along with other flavourful spices.

Paneer Do Pyaza

Paneer do pyaza is a very popular vegetarian recipe from north India. It is prepared by using paneer chunks dipped into a luscious and spicy gravy.

Nadan Kozhi Curry

Nadan Kozhi Curry is a traditional Kerala-style chicken curry which is made with succulent chicken chunks simmered in flavourful coconut oil or milk followed by some aromatic lightly roasted spices.

Mango Chicken Curry

Traditionally referred to as pacha manga curry, it is a combination of coconut, curry leaves, mustard seeds, raw mango and tender, juicy pieces of chicken.

Khatte Lehsuni Aloo

Garlic flavour tangy potato curry that every hot and spicy lover should try. Enjoy the best flavour potato curry with flavour of garlic sauteed in tangy spices for a heavenly delight.

Churumuri

A south-Indian version of bhel puri, indulge in the flavours of tantalising spices, puffed rice and crunchy peanuts with this mouth-watering snack from Mysore.
